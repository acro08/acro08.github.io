## Hoy

hace un rato sali por la compra, como de costumbre en bicicleta, un dia de haber regresado al pueblo, sin novedades, pasó una semana sin mi pero nada ha cambiado. 
Solo una cosa, las lluvias pararon, llevo medio año esperando que paren, que me den un respiro, una semana seca para poder impermeabilizar. Resulta que la temporada seca empieza justo cuando me tuve que ir y ahora regreso, con mis pendientes aun sin atender y ahora con otro mas que no puedo ignorar, ni dejarlo bajo la alfombra del olvido en la justificacion de que tengo que esperar a que deje de llover. 

Sin mas, una semana mas regresa a este ciclo que estoy construyendo, una rutina sin estructura, la entiendo como rutina por sentirse ajena a mi, adjudicandomela para reclamar sus resultados.

## Birds
En mi gusto habitan unas pocas canciones, que reflejan de mi algo particular.
Una admiracion, por alguien, a veces con rostro, nombre, voz; a veces existe como idea solamente.
Un deseo de ser aceptado por alguien que admiro, una mirada que valide mi existir a pesar de no encajar con su concepcion del mundo.
Una rebeldia ante el mundo, incluso ante mi renegarme de hacer sentido, de valer para algo, de renunciar entender en favor de sentir.

## Coldplay
De este sentimiento surge mi gusto por ellos.
I wouldnt dare to say its a guilty pleasure, it's a broadly apreciated band, tho it's not what i would understand as my taste or what im familiar with, at least not what i like about their work.
I think they have my favourite film, a documentary compilation of recordings from their career journey up to their A head full of dreams album.
there, gets portrayed, in their terms how had been their life journey, and how come the changes in their music came to be, very lightly and not much in detail. Nevertheless a message came through me, see, that guy martin has a way of interpret the world that amazed me once i payed atention to it. 
the guy doesn't have the urge to make a lot of sense nor being rational in his work, but for the better take a messagge across with more than just words that have a chain of signifiers and get interpreted straight as they are. 
This band speaks with sound with a lot more of intention than words, a thing that i can't really put in words. but i learned to understand by hearing and paying atention with more than just my mind.
Has been very challenging, this is not a thing im comfortable, used to, that comes outloudly in a natural way.
This is one great finding
